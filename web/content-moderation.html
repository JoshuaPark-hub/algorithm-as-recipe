Content Moderation Algorithm

A recipe for deciding what should disappear.

Prep Time: Instant or Delayed | Difficulty: Context-Dependent | Serves: Platform Policy

Ingredients

  • User-generated content
  • Community guidelines
  • Platform policies
  • Automated detection models
  • Flagged reports
  • Keyword databases
  • Image recognition systems
  • Human reviewers

Instructions

  1. Scan uploaded content.
  2. Compare content against policy database.
  3. Detect flagged keywords or visual patterns.
  4. Assign automated risk score.
  5. Escalate ambiguous cases to human reviewers.
  6. Remove, restrict, or label the content.

Final Dish

A curated and policy-compliant platform feed.

Some voices are removed. Others remain visible.

Reflection

Who defines what is harmful? What happens to context? Can neutrality truly exist in moderation?
