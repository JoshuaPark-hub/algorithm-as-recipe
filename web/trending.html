Trending Algorithm

A recipe for manufacturing popularity.

Prep Time: Real-time | Difficulty: Dynamic | Serves: Entire platform

Ingredients

  • Number of recent interactions
  • Rate of engagement growth
  • Shares per minute
  • Watch time acceleration
  • Hashtag clustering
  • Geographic activity spikes
  • Influencer amplification

Instructions

  1. Monitor real-time platform activity.
  2. Detect sudden spikes in engagement.
  3. Measure growth velocity.
  4. Compare against baseline averages.
  5. Amplify high-velocity content.
  6. Push selected items to the Trending section.

Key Mechanism

Trending is not based solely on total popularity, but on the speed of increase.

↑ 245% engagement growth in 3 hours → Content becomes amplified.

Final Dish

A curated list of “what everyone is watching right now.”

Popularity becomes self-reinforcing: the more visibility content receives, the more engagement it generates.

Reflection

Is content trending because people genuinely prefer it? Or does it become desirable because it has been amplified?
