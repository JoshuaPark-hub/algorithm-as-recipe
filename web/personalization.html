Personalization Engine

A recipe for shaping your unique feed.

Prep Time: Continuous | Difficulty: Adaptive | Serves: Individual users

Ingredients

  • Viewing history
  • Click patterns
  • Watch time duration
  • Scroll speed
  • Interaction frequency
  • Saved content
  • Social graph connections
  • Behavioral predictions

Instructions

  1. Track user interactions over time.
  2. Identify recurring behavioral patterns.
  3. Construct evolving preference profile.
  4. Predict likely interests.
  5. Re-rank and filter content feed.
  6. Continuously update model with new data.

Final Dish

A content feed that feels uniquely tailored to you.

The more you interact, the more precisely the system adapts.

Personalization creates comfort — but may quietly narrow your horizon.

Reflection

Is this personalization or containment? Are you discovering content — or is it discovering you? What remains unseen?
